Real Estate Calculator

Simple IRR and Cash Flow projection

How to use this tool:

•  For rates in orange, switch to fixed annual amounts by using absolute values greater than 1.
e.g. 1000 = $1000 per year; .05 = 5% of home value per year.

•  All rates are annualized unless denoted otherwise.
•  Profit to date is expressed excluding your down payment, which is considered outside as an initial investment.
•  Inflation is not considered, but can be accurately represented by subtracting the inflation rate from the appreciation rate.
•  "Years b/w rent adjustment" denotes how often rent is raised, e.g. every 1, 2, 3... years. Constant rents will ignore this property, but both constant and variable rents will be affected by vacancy rates.
